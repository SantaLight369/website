Help/Guides



Need some help?

Learning new information about your event before you start may be challenging. Going to the official TSA website helps students begin smoothly on their project.

If you're always unsure what to do, contact your TSA officers and/or your teacher advisor to get more information. They are always here to help.

Our SFHS TSA canvas page contains most of the information and links needed for projects and events (examples of what information and links are listed in Guides).

List of Guides

Here is a list of guides that can support your projects all the way:

  • TSA Competitive Events Guide (can be located on SFHS Canvas TSA page)
  • Official Website Event Descriptions
  • The READ READ READ document is a MUST. (This is also located on the Canvas page). This can help you with submission information and other extra advice for your event.
  • For the people doing an event that has a theme/problem, the link for this annually can be found here (scroll down to the high school section).
  • Here are some tips to get you going: Start early as soon as you can, don't be afraid to ask for help, and follow directions along the way.
