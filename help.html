Help

Learning new information about your event before you start may be challenging. Going to the official TSA website helps students begin smoothly on their project.

If you're unsure of what to do, contact your TSA officers and/or your TSA advisor to get more information. They are always here to help.

Our SFHS TSA Canvas page contains most of the information needed for projects and events (examples of what information and links are listed in Guides).
